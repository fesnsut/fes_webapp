FLAGSHIP BUSINESS CONCLAVE

/01

Consilium is NSUT’s premier business conclave, celebrated for its innovative events and extensive outreach. It offers a platform for participants to engage in diverse challenges like case studies, stock market simulations, fintech hackathons, and strategy-based competitions. With participation from institutions like IITs, IIMs, and DU, Consilium is a hub for fostering collaboration, learning, and leadership.

UNIQUE EVENT
EXPERIENCES

/02

Consilium isn't just another business conclave; it's an experience designed to challenge and inspire. Each event is crafted to simulate real-world scenarios, from solving intricate business problems in NSBC to developing fintech solutions in Fintechstico. The focus is on creating impactful and engaging experiences that sharpen analytical and decision-making skills.

RECRUITMENT PROCESS

Attend Orientation

/01

Begin your journey by attending the FES Orientation session, where you'll learn about our vision, activities, and how you can be part of our dynamic community.

Fill Recruitment Form

/02

After the Orientation, a recruitment form will be shared. This form is your first step in showcasing your interests, skills, and passion for finance and economics.

First Round of PI

/03

Shortlisted candidates will participate in the first round of personal interviews with our members. This round assesses your knowledge, enthusiasm, and alignment with our objectives.

Second Round of PI

/04

Successful candidates from the second round will move on to the final interview, where we delve deeper into your potential contributions to FES.

Wait for Results

/05

The results will be announced within two weeks. If selected, you’ll officially join the FES family and start your journey of growth and learning!

FAQs

What is the Finance & Economics Society (FES)?

FES is a student-led society at NSUT dedicated to fostering knowledge, skills, and innovation in finance, economics, and business through events, workshops, and publications.

Who can join FES?

FES is open to all NSUT students, regardless of their field of study, who are passionate about finance, economics, or related fields and wish to make a meaningful contribution to the society.

How can I join FES?

To join FES, attend the Orientation session, fill out the recruitment form, and successfully complete the recruitment process, which includes two rounds of personal interviews.

What kind of events does FES organize?

FES organizes a wide range of events, including Consilium (our flagship business conclave), case competitions, policy discussions, fintech hackathons, quizzes, and workshops on financial literacy and investment.

What are the benefits of joining FES?

As a member of FES, you’ll gain hands-on experience, networking opportunities, leadership skills, and exposure to real-world challenges in finance and economics. You’ll also have access to resources like workshops, eBooks, and industry connections.
