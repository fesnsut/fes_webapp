OC Recruitment CONSILIUM

Consilium, the flagship business conclave of FES, is a confluence of innovation, strategy, and intellect. With events ranging from fintech hackathons to policy case studies, it attracts over 3,000 teams annually from premier institutions like IITs, IIMs, and DU. This dynamic conclave not only fosters creativity and problem-solving but also bridges academia and industry through engaging discussions, workshops, and competitions. Consilium has proudly hosted eminent speakers like Suresh Prabhu, Sandeep Jain, and CA Rachana Ranade, making it one of the most prestigious business events in the Northern Circuit.
