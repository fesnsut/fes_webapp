CONSILIUM'25

Organizing Committee Recruitment

The Finance and Economics Society (FES) of NSUT proudly invites you to join the organizing committee for the 11th edition of our annual flagship event, Consilium. This three-day business conclave is a premier platform for learning, innovation, and collaboration. It begins with an inspiring Speaker Session featuring influential leaders from diverse fields such as finance, entrepreneurship, and entertainment. This is followed by a range of inter-institute competitions that test participants’ skills in business, finance, economics, strategy, and technology.

What are the benefits
of joining the Consilium OC?

Joining the organizing committee offers a unique and rewarding experience. It provides hands-on exposure to event management, including planning, execution, and problem-solving, which are highly valued skills across industries. You’ll have the chance to work closely with a motivated team, fostering collaboration and leadership abilities. Additionally, being part of the organizing team enhances your resume and showcases your initiative and ability to manage high-profile events. The platform also helps you gain visibility within your institution and among professionals, adding value to your personal and professional growth.

01/

Unparalleled exposure and experience

BEING PART OF OC

The organizing committee of Consilium is a dynamic environment where you can develop a variety of skills. You’ll be involved in tasks such as planning timelines, strategizing marketing campaigns, coordinating with participants, and managing event-day logistics. These activities enhance problem-solving abilities, improve organizational skills, and sharpen your communication and negotiation techniques. Moreover, working under deadlines and managing challenges helps build resilience and adaptability, preparing you to excel in any professional setting. Each task contributes to a well-rounded skill set that goes beyond textbooks and theory.

Build a Professional Network

Consilium attracts participants from top institutions and industry professionals, offering you a chance to network with like-minded individuals and experts. Being on the organizing side allows you to interact directly with guest speakers, judges, and participants, opening doors for mentorship and future collaborations.

02/

Fun and fulfilling experience

BEING PART OF OC

Beyond the skills and exposure, Consilium promises to be an enjoyable and memorable experience. Collaborate with peers, tackle challenges, and celebrate your success as a team. The friendships and memories you create along the way will be cherished long after the event concludes.

So, what are you waiting for?

Take this golden opportunity to be part of something extraordinary. Together, let’s make the 11th edition of Consilium a resounding success!
